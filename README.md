# Xeenom-Sales-Analysis--Excel
The Xeenom Sales Performance Dashboard provides a comprehensive, at-a-glance view of the company’s commercial performance across time, geography, customer segments, and product categories. Designed for strategic monitoring and quick decision-making, the dashboard consolidates key sales metrics and trends into a single interactive interface.

## Dashboard Breakdown
At the top level, it highlights core KPIs including Total Sales ($2.30M), Quantity Sold (5,009 units), Total Orders (37,873), and Average Discount (15.62%), with year-based filtering (2011–2014) to enable period comparisons and performance tracking. Mini bar visuals beneath each KPI show monthly patterns, helping users quickly identify fluctuations and seasonality.

The “By Month” line chart tracks revenue trends throughout the year and compares current performance against the previous year. This allows management to detect growth momentum, dips, and peak months—such as strong performance in September and November—supporting forecasting and seasonal planning.

### Geographic performance is presented in two ways:

A Top 5 States ranking (including California, New York, Texas, Washington, and Pennsylvania), highlighting the company’s strongest markets.

A U.S. map visual that displays regional sales distribution, making concentration patterns easy to interpret visually.

Customer insights are provided through segment analysis (Consumer, Corporate, Home Office), showing Consumer as the leading revenue contributor. The Product Category donut chart breaks down revenue across Furniture, Office Supplies, and Technology, helping leadership understand product mix and contribution balance.

Additionally, the Segment Distribution by Region visualization compares how customer segments perform across West, East, Central, and South regions, supporting regional strategy optimization.

### Overall, this dashboard achieves three main objectives:

##### Performance Monitoring – 
Tracks financial and operational KPIs in real time.

##### Trend & Comparative Analysis – 
Identifies growth patterns across months and years.

##### Strategic Insight – 
Reveals geographic concentration, customer behavior, and product performance to guide expansion and diversification decisions.

#### In essence:
The Xeenom Sales Dashboard transforms raw sales data into actionable business intelligence for leadership and operational teams.
